About the Project

This project is an interactive wiki page dedicated to the game. Hollow Knight: Silksong - continuation of the famous Metroidvania Hollow Knight from the studio Team Cherry.

The project's goal is to provide structured information about the game: plot, characters, locations, mechanics, items, enemies, and tips for completing the game.

Project Structure

  • Main Page: Project overview and introductory information.
  • Gameplay: Description of game mechanics, skills, and combat system.
  • Story: Hollow Knight's plot, characters, and world.
  • Guides: Tips and walkthroughs for players.
  • Media: A gallery of screenshots, art, and trailers.
  • Contributors: Information about the project development team.

Hollow Knight: Silksong

Hollow Knight: Silksong - is a 2D Metroidvania adventure where the player controls the heroine Hornet, who finds herself in a mysterious land of silk and song. The player will explore new worlds, battle over 150 types of enemies, and uncover the secrets of ancient kingdoms.

  • Genre: Metroidvania, Platformer
  • Developer: Team Cherry
  • Platforms: PC, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, Xbox
  • Release day: September 4, 2025
